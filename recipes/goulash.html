Goulash

Home Page

Description

Are you familiar with Goulash? If not, let me introduce you to this old fashioned recipe! This recipe is an American Goulash that’s great for busy weeknights, but I love to take this recipe to potlucks and parties because it makes a ton and is super affordable. It’s also great for picky eaters, I haven’t met one kid who doesn’t love this dish!

Goulash is filled with ground beef, onion and bell peppers along with macaroni noodles in a tomato-based sauce. I like to stir in some sharp cheddar cheese at the end to give it a creamy texture. My family loves it!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 3 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans diced tomato
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 3 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp seasoned salt
  • 2 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 cups dry macaroni noodles
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. added olive oil to large pot of water over medium heat
  2. add onion, bell pepper into ground beef cook till no longer pink
  3. drain ground beef then add garlic
  4. pour tomato sauce and diced tomatoes in to a pot
  5. add beef broth, worcestershire sauce,
    seasoned salt,italian seasoning,
    bay leaves and dry macaroni noodles in to pot bring to boil
  6. str until pasta tender
  7. remove bay leaves
  8. str in cheddar cheese
  9. cool and serve
