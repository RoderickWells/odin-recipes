Apple Crisp

Description

This apple crisp recipe is a simple yet delicious fall dessert that's great served warm with vanilla ice cream.

Making irresistible apple crisp is surprisingly easy. You just need a good recipe â€” and that's where we come in! This top-rated apple crisp recipe with a sweet oat topping is sure to satisfy everyone at your table.

Ingredients

  • 10 peeled,cored and sliced apples
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 cup quick oats
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. place apples in a baking dish
  2. sprinkle apples with white sugar,flour and cinnamon
  3. drizzle water over the apples
  4. make the oat topping, then sprinkle it over apples
  5. bake until the topping is golden brown and the apple mixture is bubbly
